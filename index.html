About Asha Physiotherapy

Our mission, our values, and our team.

Our Mission: Hope and Healing

Our mission is to provide the highest quality physiotherapy services with compassion and care. We are dedicated to helping our patients achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized treatment plans and patient education. 'Asha', meaning 'hope', is at the core of everything we do.

We believe in empowering our patients by giving them the knowledge and tools to manage their conditions and prevent future injuries. Your health is our priority, and we are committed to supporting you every step of the way.

Our Services

A comprehensive range of treatments to meet your needs.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Specialized programs to help athletes recover from injuries and enhance performance safely.

Post-Operative Care

Structured rehabilitation to ensure a smooth and successful recovery after surgery.

Back & Neck Pain Treatment

Effective solutions for chronic or acute pain in the back and neck, using manual therapy and exercises.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Care for individuals with neurological conditions like stroke, Parkinson's, or spinal cord injuries.

Geriatric Care

Improving mobility, balance, and quality of life for our elderly patients.

Home Visits

We offer physiotherapy services in the comfort of your own home for those with mobility challenges.
