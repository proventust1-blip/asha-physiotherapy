ABOUT ASHA ORTHOPEDIA

Founded on the principle of providing compassionate and high-quality care, ASHA ORTHOPEDIA is a leading physiotherapy and rehabilitation centre in Nepal. Our team of certified physiotherapists is dedicated to helping patients of all ages overcome physical challenges, recover from injuries, and improve their quality of life.

We believe in a patient-centric approach, creating customized treatment plans that address your unique needs and goals.
